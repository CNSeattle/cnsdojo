🥷 Clubs Are Back!

Weekly after-school clubs where Ninjas level up their skills, make friends, and have a blast.

Minecraft & Roblox Clubs begin April 1st & 2nd and run through June 10th & 11th.

Each club is a 1-hour drop-in where Ninjas get a safe space to work on related games, have questions answered, and connect with other club goers.

Laptops are provided, but you're welcome to bring your own.

Have questions? Email us at seattlewa@codeninjas.com or simply reply to this email.

⛏️ Minecraft Club

Wednesdays • 3:00–4:00 PM

Ninjas master modding with MCreator, build their own custom worlds, and get expert guidance from our Senseis. It's the perfect space to show off your mods, share your creations, and level up your Minecraft skills alongside fellow creators.

🎮 Roblox Club

Thursdays • 6:00–7:00 PM

In our Roblox Club, Ninjas dive into Roblox Studio to design and build their very own games from scratch. They'll explore game design principles and learn to code with Lua, all in a safe, supportive environment. This isn't about playing Roblox online; it's about learning how games are made.
