About the Dojo

Code Ninjas Seattle is a fun, safe space where kids learn to code by building their own video games. We teach coding and computer science in a way that feels exciting and hands-on — turning screen time into skill-building time.

We’re both an education center and an extracurricular activity, designed to fit busy family schedules. Our flexible, drop-in model means there’s no need to sign up for a specific class time — simply choose the days and times that work best for you.

Our Senseis guide each Ninja through engaging projects that build foundational skills in coding, computer science, problem-solving, logic, and creativity. Kids progress at their own pace while gaining real-world tech skills and confidence along the way.

Beyond our core program, we also offer summer camps, clubs, year-round day camps, and unforgettable birthday parties — making Code Ninjas Seattle a year-round destination for learning and fun.

⭐ Dojo Owners

Geo & Chelsea

Code Ninjas Seattle is owned and operated by Geo and Chelsea, longtime Ballard residents of 15 years. With a 5th grader and a preschooler of their own, they're passionate about creativity, education, and building community. When Geo's creative work paused during the pandemic, the opportunity to launch Code Ninjas in Seattle emerged and they jumped in. For the past four years, they've been dedicated to creating a fun, welcoming space where kids can discover, build, and grow.
