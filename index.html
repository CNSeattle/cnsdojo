Meet the Senseis

The team behind Code Ninjas Seattle

⭐ Dojo Owners

Geo & Chelsea

Code Ninjas Seattle is owned and operated by Geo and Chelsea, longtime Ballard residents of 15 years. With a 5th grader and a preschooler of their own, they're passionate about creativity, education, and building community. When Geo's creative work paused during the pandemic, the opportunity to launch Code Ninjas in Seattle emerged and they jumped in. For the past four years, they've been dedicated to creating a fun, welcoming space where kids can discover, build, and grow.

About the Dojo

Code Ninjas Seattle is a locally owned and operated dojo in the heart of Ballard, Seattle. We help kids ages 7–14 discover the power of coding, game design, and computational thinking.

Our ninjas earn belts as they level up through our CREATE program — from beginner block coding all the way to advanced JavaScript and real game builds. Every session is guided by our expert Code Senseis.

Whether it's after-school programs, summer camps, clubs, or Parent's Night Out — there's something for every young creator at the dojo.
