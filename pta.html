SCHOOLS &
PTA PARTNERS

We love partnering with local schools and PTAs!

Our team works with your community to support both fundraising and student learning. We can help create customized fundraising campaigns and bring fun, accessible coding and STEM programs directly to your school — including after-school classes, enrichment programs, and special events.

Let's connect and build something great together!

Fundraising Partnerships

Support Your PTA While Supporting Student Learning

We partner with schools and PTAs to create easy, community-driven fundraising opportunities. Through customized signup drives, a portion of new student enrollment is donated back to your organization.

We also host spirit nights, grade-level events, and buy-in parties at our North Seattle center — providing the space, curriculum, and fun while your school raises funds.

In-School Learning & Enrichment

Coding & STEM Programs Brought to Your School

We bring engaging coding and STEM education directly to your students. Our CSTA-aligned programs support enrichment, after-school learning, and extracurricular opportunities.

We provide both the instructor and curriculum, with flexible program lengths designed to fit your school's needs.

Built by Parents, for Schools

We Understand Your School Community

As parents and PTA members ourselves, we understand how Seattle Public Schools operate and the realities of working within elementary and middle school communities.

Our goal is to make partnerships simple, collaborative, and valuable for students, educators, and families alike.
